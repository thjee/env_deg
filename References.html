The Prophecy, tale of nine spirits that warn of the perils of neglecting the environment. After 30 years living abroad, photographer Fabrice Monteiro returned to Africa to find his homeland an environmental disaster. Inspired by an idea to educate children, Monteiro teamed up with fashion designer Doulsy to create “The Prophecy,” a tale of nine spirits that warn of the perils of neglecting the environment. The project has particular resonance this year with the recent adoption of the UN’s new Sustainable Development Goals and the upcoming climate change conference where a global agreement is expected.
